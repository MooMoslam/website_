Our Services

"Our core services represent the heart of what we do — three powerful services designed to elevate your experience and drive meaningful results."

Robot Control

"We have full control over the robot's movement and speed, with automatic halting upon obstacle detection. Moreover, we can seamlessly activate any disinfection mode, whether UV or spray, and ensure immediate robot shutdown in case of an emergency."

Monitoring

"We provide seamless monitoring of both the room’s temperature and humidity to assess optimal disinfection conditions, while also enabling real-time tracking of the robot’s status, including its temperature, humidity, battery level, and water tank capacity."

Web Server

"Our web server is designed with simplicity and clarity in mind, providing an intuitive and seamless user experience. With a clean, straightforward interface, it ensures effortless access and control, allowing users to interact with the system with ease and efficiency."
