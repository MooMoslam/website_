About

We are a group of creative biomedical engineers seeking to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology. In our initial study, we successfully classified chronic kidney diseases using machine learning techniques. We also successfully conducted deep learning research to improve the identification of parasites in microscopic images, overcoming challenging obstacles in this process. We now intend to launch a hardware-focused project to advance our knowledge and gain practical experience in various aspects of biomedical engineering.
